About Me

Hi, I’m Jean-Luc A. Saint-Fleur—a data-driven problem solver, business strategist, and advocate for diversity in technology.

My journey has taken me across industries, from hospitality to the nonprofit sector, where I help organizations make data-driven decisions that create real impact. I specialize in leveraging AI, machine learning, and data analytics to drive efficiency, optimize financial strategies, and uncover meaningful insights.

Beyond data, I’m passionate about teamwork, problem-solving, and strategy—values I developed both in my professional career and as a former college soccer player. Just like in sports, I believe in adapting, collaborating, and pushing boundaries to achieve success in business, technology, and life.

I speak Haitian Creole, French, and English fluently and am currently learning Spanish. I approach learning new programming languages the same way—embracing challenges, continuous growth, and new perspectives.

Whether it’s building machine learning models, optimizing financial strategies, or solving business problems, I thrive on turning data into actionable insights. Let’s connect and make an impact together!
